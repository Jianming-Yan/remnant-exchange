Outcome Options

Calling Tips

  • Call between 9–11am — owners are in the shop and not yet deep in the day
  • Log every call, even no-answers — it shows your activity and keeps you organized
  • If you get voicemail, leave a message and log it — call back in 2 days
  • Note objections in the Notes column — they'll help us improve the pitch
  • Goal: 10 calls per day minimum. Expect 2–3 interested per 10 calls.
  • A "not interested" today can become a "yes" in 3 months — keep the list

RemnantExchange.org is a live marketplace growing across the U.S. We're looking for 2–3 ambitious people to help design and execute the campaigns that grow it.

The opportunity
Most stone fabricators — granite shops, marble yards, countertop studios — have thousands of dollars in leftover slab sitting in their yard. Buyers need that exact material and have no way to find it. We built the platform to connect them. Now we need people to help us grow it city by city across the U.S.

This isn't a one-task internship. You'll work directly with the founder to plan campaigns, test what works, and own the results. If you help build this, you'll have equity in what you built.

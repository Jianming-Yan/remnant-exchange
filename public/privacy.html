Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Remnant Exchange ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website remnantexchange.org. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you register or use the platform:

  • Name and business name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Photos of stone remnants you upload
  • Listing details (dimensions, material type, location)

We do not collect payment information. We do not track browsing behavior or use advertising cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your fabricator account
  • To display your listings to buyers on the platform
  • To send you account-related emails (verification, approval, listing notifications)
  • To forward buyer messages to you via email when someone contacts you about a listing
  • To review new fabricator applications for approval

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. What Buyers Can See

When a buyer views a listing, they can see your business name and the details of your listing. Your personal email address and phone number are never displayed publicly. Buyers contact you through a message form, and we forward the message to your email on their behalf.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Resend — sends transactional emails on our behalf
  • Turso — cloud database hosting for account and listing data
  • Railway — web hosting and server infrastructure
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — used optionally to identify stone type from photos you upload. Photos are sent to Anthropic's API and are not stored by them beyond the request.

5. Data Retention

Your account and listing data are retained as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, contact us at info.remnantexchange@gmail.com and we will remove it within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info.remnantexchange@gmail.com.

7. Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed passwords. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Remnant Exchange is intended for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users by email if material changes are made. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

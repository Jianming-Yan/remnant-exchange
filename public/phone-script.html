Phone Outreach Script

Remnant Exchange — use this for cold calls to stone fabricators

Step 1

Opening — First 10 Seconds

Be warm, direct, and respectful of their time. Don't ask "How are you?" — fabricators are busy.

"Hi, my name is Jianming, I'm calling from Remnant Exchange. Is the owner available for just 30 seconds?"

If owner is not available:

"No problem — can I get their name and best time to reach them? I'll call back then."
Step 2

The 30-Second Pitch

Once you have the owner, keep it short. One problem, one solution, one ask.

"I run a free website called Remnant Exchange — remnantexchange.org — where stone fabricators list their leftover slab remnants for sale. Buyers find you, you clear your shop floor, and it costs nothing. Takes about 5 minutes to sign up. Would you be open to taking a quick look?"
Step 3A

If They're Interested

"Great — go to remnantexchange.org — that's dot ORG, not dot com. Click Register, it's free. Once you're in, post a photo of one remnant and you're live. Any questions, call me at 617-606-5840."

Then follow up by text immediately: "Hi [Name], it's Jianming from Remnant Exchange. Here's the link: remnantexchange.org — free signup, takes 5 min."

Step 3B

If They Need to Think

"Totally understand — there's no rush. It's completely free, no contract, so there's really nothing to lose. Can I text you the link? You can look whenever you have a minute."

Log the follow-up date and call back in 3–5 days.

Step 4

Handling Objections

"I don't have time for this."
"I understand — signing up takes 5 minutes and you never have to touch it again unless a buyer contacts you. Can I text you the link for when you have a slow moment?"
"How much does it cost?"
"It's completely free to list. No monthly fee, no commission, no credit card. We're building the network right now and we want fabricators on board."
"I need to ask my boss / owner."
"Of course — it's free and there's no contract, so there's nothing to approve. You can sign up and show them — it takes 5 minutes. But if you'd like, I can call back when the owner is available."
"We already sell our remnants."
"That's great — this just gives you another channel to reach more buyers. More buyers means faster sales and less stone sitting in your shop. And it's free, so there's no downside to listing."
"I'm not interested."
"No problem at all. If anything changes, the site is remnantexchange.org — free anytime. Thank you for your time!"
Voicemail

If You Get Voicemail

"Hi, this is Jianming calling from Remnant Exchange — a free website where stone fabricators sell their leftover remnants. I'd love to tell you about it. Please call me back at 617-606-5840, or visit remnantexchange.org — that's dot ORG. Thank you!"

Call back in 2 days if no response.

Tips

Calling Tips

⏰
Best time to call: 9–11am and 2–4pm. Avoid Mondays before 10am and Fridays after 3pm.
📱
Text after every call: Send the link immediately after a positive call — while they still remember you.
📋
Log every call: Even no-answers. It keeps you organized and shows your activity.
🔄
Call back 3 times before marking as not interested. Most people need 2–3 touches.
🎯
Goal: 20–30 calls per day. Expect 3–5 interested per 20 calls.
🌐
Always say "dot ORG" — not dot com. Repeat it twice if needed.

Remnant Exchange — Confidential Personal Arrangement

Company: Remnant Exchange  |  Jianming Yan, Founder

Date: _______________________________________________

1. Background

Remnant Exchange (remnantexchange.org) is a free online marketplace where stone fabricators list, buy, and sell leftover stone remnants. Daniela works with over 60 stone fabricator accounts and is well-positioned to introduce Remnant Exchange to fabricators who would benefit from the platform.

This is a personal arrangement between Jianming Yan and Daniela, independent of her employer. Nothing in this agreement requires Daniela to act on behalf of or in the name of her employer.

2. Partner Role

Daniela agrees to:

  • Introduce Remnant Exchange to stone fabricators in her network on a voluntary, as-available basis
  • Refer fabricators to remnantexchange.org and encourage them to register and post their remnants
  • Notify Jianming Yan of fabricators she has referred so referrals can be tracked and credited

There is no minimum referral requirement. Daniela participates at her own discretion.

3. Compensation

Type Amount Condition
Referral Commission $20 per fabricator Fabricator must post at least one real remnant listing. Credit is given whether the fabricator is newly registered or was already registered but inactive — as long as Daniela is the one who encouraged them to post
Equity Stake 10 shares per active fabricator Shares awarded at IPO or liquidity event. Accrues for every fabricator Daniela refers who becomes active on the platform

Referral commissions will be paid via Venmo, Zelle, or check within 14 days of a referred fabricator's first active posting. Jianming Yan will maintain a running record of credited referrals and share it with Daniela upon request.

4. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep the terms of this arrangement confidential. Daniela agrees not to disclose the existence or details of Remnant Exchange to competitors or third parties beyond what is necessary to make referrals. Jianming Yan agrees not to disclose Daniela's involvement to her employer or clients without her consent.

5. No Exclusivity

This is a non-exclusive arrangement. Jianming Yan may work with other referral partners. Daniela is free to refer or not refer at her own discretion and is not bound to any exclusivity.

6. Independent Arrangement

Daniela participates in this arrangement as an individual, not as a representative of her employer. She is responsible for ensuring her participation does not violate any obligations to her employer. Remnant Exchange is not responsible for any employment-related consequences.

7. Term

This agreement is ongoing and may be ended by either party at any time with written or verbal notice. All earned and unpaid commissions will be paid upon termination.

8. Agreement

By signing below, both parties agree to the terms of this referral partnership.

Internship Agreement

Remnant Exchange — Business Development & Marketing Intern

Company: Remnant Exchange  |  Jianming Yan, Founder

Intern:

Start Date:

Duration: Ongoing, part-time (or until terminated by either party)

1. Role & Responsibilities

The intern will serve as a Business Development & Marketing Intern, with primary responsibilities including:

  • Researching stone fabricator businesses — finding contact names, phone numbers, and email addresses
  • Making outbound calls to introduce Remnant Exchange and explain its value to fabricators
  • Assisting with fabricator account creation and onboarding
  • Following up with fabricators to ensure they have logged in and posted their remnants
  • Supporting other marketing and business development tasks as assigned

2. Compensation

The intern will be compensated as follows:

Type Amount Condition
Base Pay $15.00 / hour Paid weekly based on hours worked
Performance Bonus $15 per fabricator Fabricator must have at least one real remnant listing posted on the platform
Milestone Bonus $200 per milestone Earned for every 20 active fabricators (with real postings) cumulatively reached

Payment will be made via Venmo, Zelle, or check, as agreed upon between both parties. Hours must be logged and submitted weekly for approval.

3. Hours & Schedule

This is a flexible, part-time internship. Expected hours, schedule, and working arrangement (in-person, remote, or hybrid) will be agreed upon separately. The intern is not guaranteed a minimum number of hours per week.

4. Independent Contractor Status

The intern will be engaged as an independent contractor, not an employee. The intern is responsible for their own taxes. A Form 1099 will be issued if total compensation exceeds $600 in a calendar year.

5. Confidentiality

The intern agrees to keep all business information, customer data, and platform details confidential during and after the internship. This includes fabricator contact lists, business strategy, and any proprietary information learned during the internship.

6. At-Will Arrangement

Either party may end this arrangement at any time, for any reason, with reasonable notice. All earned and unpaid compensation will be paid upon termination.

7. Agreement

By signing below, both parties agree to the terms of this internship arrangement.
